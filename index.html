Background

This past semester, I had the opportunity to evaluate how my online practices intertwine with digital data. Specifically, how different social media platforms affect my emotion. Further, I designed a data visualization and evaluated how my digital data resonates with a social issue. This webpage conveys this process and what I have learned.

Data Collection

The first assignment was data collection where I analyzed and recorded how different social media platforms affect my different emotions.The three social media platoforms were Instagram,Facebook and Snapchat while the different emotions were Laughter,Excited,Happiness,Neutral,Sad,Jealous Stressed. Over the span of one week, every time I would look at the different platforms I would record how long I was on the platform, what I saw, how different post made me feel including notes as to why I felt the way I did and the relationship to the person who posted. Data Visualization

In order to further analyze my data, I designed a categorical graph data visualization using Inkscape. When comparing the different data points and clusters I discovered that Twitter and Facebook were the social media platforms that had the biggest impact on my emotion. Also, strangers and acquaintances had the most impact on my emotion whether that be positive or negative.
