Background

This past semester, I had the opportunity to evaluate how my online practices intertwine with digital data. Specifically, how different social media platforms affect my emotion. Further, I designed a data visualization and evaluated how my digital data resonates with a social issue. This webpage conveys this process and what I have learned.

Data Collection

The first assignment was data collection where I analyzed and recorded how different social media platforms affect my different emotions.The three social media platoforms were Instagram,Facebook and Snapchat while the different emotions were Laughter,Excited,Happiness,Neutral,Sad,Jealous,Stressed. Over the span of one week, every time I would look at the different platforms I would record how long I was on the platform, where I was when I was viewing social media, the device I was viewing the sites from, my reason for using the app, what I saw, how different post made me feel including notes as to why I felt the way I did and the relationship to the person who posted. Data Visualization

In order to further analyze my data, I designed a categorical graph data visualization using Inkscape. The ranges of emotion are placed on the X-axis while the social media platform is on the y-axis. The color key defining relationsips is located at the top left corner. When comparing the different data points and clusters I discovered that the main variable that had an affect on my emotion was relationship to the person who posted.
